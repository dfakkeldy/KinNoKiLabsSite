The useful boundary

A map can tell you where.
It cannot tell you whether.

Mapped boundaries are not a survey. A notice is not a promise that a property remains in the sale. Neither the map nor the guide proves access, title, condition, value, possession, insurance, financing, or buildability.

Use them to ask better questions—then verify the current municipal source and bring the unresolved ones to the right qualified professional.
