# Visual Timer

**Visual Timer** is a clean, intuitive countdown timer built for Apple Watch and iOS.

Designed for simplicity, it helps you stay focused by providing a clear, glanceable representation of time remaining. Perfect for productivity, studying, and time management.

Available on iOS and watchOS.
