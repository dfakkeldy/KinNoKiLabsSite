# Odysseus

**Odysseus** is a self-hosted AI workspace — meant to be the self-hosted version of the UI experience you get from ChatGPT and Claude, running on your own hardware, with your own data. Local-first, privacy-first, and highly capable.

**Features:**
- **Chat & Agents:** Chat with local models or APIs, and run fully autonomous agent tasks.
- **Deep Research:** Multi-step runs that gather, read, and synthesize sources into a report.
- **Memory & Skills:** Persistent memory and skills so your agent evolves over time.
- **Email, Notes & Calendar:** Built-in triage, reminders, and CalDAV sync.
- **Mobile Friendly:** Works great on mobile as an installable PWA.
