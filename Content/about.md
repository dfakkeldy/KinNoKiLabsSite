# About Us

We are **KinNoKi Labs** — a small, focused team of software engineers and designers who believe that great tools come from deep understanding.

## What We Do

We build native applications for Apple platforms — iOS, macOS, watchOS, and visionOS. Every project starts with the same question: *what does the user actually need?* From there, we design, prototype, and ship with an emphasis on clarity, performance, and craftsmanship.

## Our Approach

- **Swift-first.** We use Swift across the stack, from apps to server-side tooling.
- **Pragmatic design.** We don't chase trends. We build interfaces that feel obvious in daily use.
- **Open source.** We share what we learn. Many of our internal tools and libraries are published under permissive licenses.

## The Team

KinNoKi Labs was founded by a group of engineers who met while working on developer tools and productivity software. We're distributed across time zones and united by a shared obsession with getting the details right.
